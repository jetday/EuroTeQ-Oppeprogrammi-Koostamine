Czech Technical University in Prague

Usage:
Fill out as much of the form as you want and click the 'Submit' button.
In the course table view, you can click on the course names to view their respective subjectmaps.
the course table view you can choose courses of your choosing to add them to your program. You can do this by clicking the checkboxes on the courses of your choosing and clicking the button 'Add Selected'.
You can view the courses you have selected by clicking the 'Selected' button.
You can click the 'Clear Selected' and 'Clear all' buttons to clear the courses you have added to your program.
